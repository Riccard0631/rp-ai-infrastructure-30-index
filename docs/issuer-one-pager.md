# RP AI Infrastructure 30 Index - Issuer Brief

## What It Is

RPAI30 is a public informational benchmark tracking 30 listed companies connected to AI infrastructure.

The index is designed to capture the infrastructure stack behind AI:

- semiconductors
- semiconductor equipment
- EDA software
- cloud platforms
- data centers
- power and cooling
- networking
- cybersecurity
- observability

## Why It May Be Interesting

AI exposure is often marketed through broad AI software or mega-cap technology baskets. RPAI30 focuses on the physical and digital infrastructure layer required to deploy AI at scale.
